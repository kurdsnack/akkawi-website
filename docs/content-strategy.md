# Content Strategy — Samir Akkawi Jewelry

## What content goes where

### Home (`/`)
The first impression. Should answer: who are you, why trust you, how do I explore.
- Hero: strong image + one-line brand statement + CTA to collections
- Trust signal: brief "30+ years" or origin story hook
- Collections preview: 2 tiles (Diamond, Gold)
- Testimonials: expand beyond the single quote on the current site — gather 3–5
- Contact strip: address, phone, WhatsApp link

### About (`/about`)
The brand story. Human, specific, credible.
- Current content (from existing site) is a solid foundation — refine the copy, don't overwrite it
- Consider adding a photo of the store or Samir

### Services (`/services`)
What you can do for the customer beyond browse-and-buy.
- Keep the 4-tile grid structure: Diamond Jewelry, Gold Jewelry, Repair & Maintenance, Appraisals & Consultancy
- Add a short paragraph expanding each service

### Policies (`/policies`)
Trust-building. Clear, scannable.
- Current policy content is good — reformat for readability
- Diamond: 30-day free return/exchange; after 30 days: 30% return fee, 15% exchange fee
- Gold: returned at market price, manufacturing and stone costs deducted
- Warranty: market-price guarantee + complimentary polishing/servicing

### Contact (`/contact`)
Clear path to reaching the store.
- Form: Name, Email, Phone, Subject, Message
- Map embed or address block (9 Shatt Al-Arab St, Um Uthaina, Amman)
- WhatsApp link (mobile-first audience)
- Social links

### Collections (`/collections`)
Hub page. Two choices: Diamond or Gold.
- Two large image tiles, each with a short description and "Explore" CTA

### Diamond collection (`/collections/diamond`)
Category index: Rings, Necklaces, Bracelets, Solitaire Rings, Earrings, Pendants

### Gold collection (`/collections/gold`)
Category index: Rings, Earrings, Bracelets, Necklaces
**Note:** This collection is entirely missing from the current site — content needs to be created from scratch or provided by the client.
