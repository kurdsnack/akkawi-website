# Brand Voice — Samir Akkawi Jewelry

## Who they are
Boutique fine jewelry store in Amman, Jordan. In business since the early 1990s. Founded on personal trust, credibility, and quality. Not a chain — a family name on the door.

## Brand positioning (from current site)
> "Unique yet classic fine gold & diamond jewelry to last a lifetime."

The brand lives at the intersection of **timeless luxury** and **personal trust**. It's not flashy or trend-driven. It's the jeweler you've known for 30 years.

---

## Tone guidelines

**Do:**
- Warm, confident, unhurried
- First-person plural ("we believe", "our craft")
- Specific over vague ("Belgian certified diamonds" not "high quality stones")
- Evoke occasions: engagements, weddings, milestones, gifts
- Mention the human behind the store where appropriate (Samir)

**Don't:**
- No hype language ("best ever", "unbeatable", "amazing deals")
- No urgency tactics ("limited time!", "only 3 left!")
- Avoid overly corporate or detached tone
- Don't use casual/Gen-Z language

## Core values (from About page)
1. High quality jewelry
2. After-sales service
3. We value our customers
4. We are a family
5. We are credible
6. We live by our principles

## Audience
Jewelry buyers in Amman for life milestones: engagements, weddings, anniversaries, birthdays. Often gifting or investing in a long-lasting piece. They care about trust and craftsmanship more than price.

---

## Language notes
- Store is in Jordan — copy should feel appropriate for a Middle Eastern audience but not exclude an international reader
- Phone and address are displayed — in-person and WhatsApp contact is a real conversion path
- No pricing on site (standard for this market segment) — lean into consultation and personal service instead
